Welcome to newtFire.org

newtFire is the project development site of Pitt-Greensburg's Center for the Digital Text, an incubator of faculty and student learning and research collaboration in the Digital Humanities. Our active projects are led and maintained by faculty, students, or alumni of the University of Pittsburgh at Greensburg, and several reflect collaborations across multiple institutions.

Our ongoing projects include:

  • Amadis in Translation
  • Pittsburgh Bicentennial Frankenstein Project
  • Digital Mitford Project
  • Digital Humanities Courses

The following featured projects began or developed in Pitt-Greensburg's coding courses:

  • Emily Dickinson: Fascicles 16 and 6
  • The Hamilton Project
  • The Lope de Vega Project
  • Nell Nelson: City Slave Girls
  • Digital Archives and Pacific Cultures
